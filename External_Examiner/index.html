Available as External Examiner for PhD & MTech Theses

I am available to serve as an external examiner for PhD and MTech thesis evaluations. My research interests for thesis evaluation include:

  • EEG Signal Analysis
  • Brain-Computer Interface (BCI)
  • Applied Machine Learning
  • Medical Image and Signal Analysis
  • XAI
  • Medical AI

The review process typically takes:

  • PhD Thesis: 2-3 months (depending on the length of the thesis and my availability)
  • MTech Thesis: 20 days to 1 month (depending on the length of the thesis and my availability)

If you're interested in having me as an examiner, please reach out to me via email with details (synopsis/summary of the thesis max. 2 pages) about the thesis work.

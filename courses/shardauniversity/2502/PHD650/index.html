📘 Syllabus (PHD650 - Theory)

  • Unit 1: Analysis of Algorithms
    • A. Algorithms analysis: P, NP, NP-Complete and NPHard problems.
    • B. Algorithm Design Paradigms, Greedy Approach.
    • C. Dynamic Programming, Approximation Algorithms.
  • Unit 2: Digital Image Processing: Techniques and Algorithm
    • A. Introduction to Digital Image, Its Application area, Binarization of a Digital Image.
    • B. Image Segmentation technique: clustering, K-means and fuzzy C-means algorithm.
    • C. Fuzzy approach and Neural Network, Problem with color images, Extraction of objects from color images.
  • Unit 3: Wireless Mobile Networks
    • A. Mobile ad-hoc and sensor networks, probability theory, traffic theory, queuing theory.
    • B. Routing: Unicast Routing, Multicast Routing, Broadcasting Routing, Hierarchically organized Networks, Energy Efficiency and QOS: Energy Efficiency, Quality of Service.
    • C. Mobile Communication Systems: Registration, Roaming, Multicasting, Security and Privacy, Optical Networking.
  • Unit 4: Machine Intelligence
    • A. Intelligent Agents, Problem-Solving Agents, Breadth-first search, Depth-first search.
    • B. Optimal Decisions in Games, Alpha–Beta Pruning, Defining Constraint Satisfaction Problems.
    • C. Constraint Propagation, Backtracking Search for CSPs, Knowledge-Based Agents.
  • Unit 5: Soft Computing
    • A. Introduction to soft computing tools; Handling Uncertainties by soft computing techniques.
    • B. Fuzzy set generalization of crisp set; Fuzzy logic; Fuzzy Mathematics-its application in engineering problems.
    • C. Genetic Algorithms- its use in handling optimization problems, Dynamics of Genetic Algorithms; Complexity issues of Genetic Algorithms.
